# Publication Strategy & Venues

## Tier 1: High-Impact Journals (Peer-Reviewed)
1.  **Journal of Artificial Societies and Social Simulation (JASSS)**
    *   *Focus*: Computational Social Science and Agent-Based Modeling.
    *   *Requirement*: High emphasis on reproducibility and model validation.
2.  **Electoral Studies**
    *   *Focus*: Electoral system design and empirical analysis of voting.
    *   *Requirement*: Strong focus on the Gallagher Index and Comparative politics.
3.  **EPJ Data Science**
    *   *Focus*: Large-scale data analysis and socio-political simulations.

## Tier 2: Pre-print & Working Papers
1.  **arXiv (CS.CY - Computers and Society)**
    *   *Benefit*: Immediate timestamping and global visibility.
2.  **SSRN (Political Science Network)**
    *   *Benefit*: Standard for Law and Economics/Political Science communities.

## Tier 3: Policy Think Tanks (India-Specific)
1.  **Observer Research Foundation (ORF)**: Target the "Political Economy" or "Governance" series.
2.  **Centre for Policy Research (CPR)**: Ideal for the hybrid-PR debate.
3.  **Vidhi Centre for Legal Policy**: Best for engaging with the Law Commission's recommendations.
